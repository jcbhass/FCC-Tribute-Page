Carl Larsson

Renowned Painter of Idyllic Swedish Life

"...these paintings are of course a very genuine expression of my personality, of my deepest feelings, of all my limitless love for my wife and children."

Wikipedia Page

  • 1853 - Born in Gamla Stan
  • 1866 - Attended Principskolan, the preparatory deparment of the Art Academy
  • 1869 - Attended the Art Academy
  • 1877 - Traveled to Paris
  • 1882 - Moved to Grez-zur-Loing, Scandinavian artist community outside of Paris
  • 1883 - Married Karin Bergöö
  • 1888 - Was given Lilla Hyttnäs by Karin's father and began using water colors
  • 1891 - Moved into Lilla Hyttnäs
  • 1896 - Painted for National Museet
  • 1919 - Died in Falun
  • 1992 - Midvinterblot finally placed in National Museet
